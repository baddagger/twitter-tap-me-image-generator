1. Configure Input Images

Best effect is achieved when the cover is a part of the hidden image (aligned), and the rest of the cover remains transparent (transparent areas trigger Alpha thresholding to fully hide the background, while solid areas will blend both layers).

2. Generation Settings

Set the aspect ratio of the output image. Different ratios will be cropped accordingly.

Resolution must be an exact multiple of Twitter preview sizes (e.g., 1400px or 2112px) so that the downsampling grid aligns perfectly with the interlocking pattern.

Lossless keeps full quality. Color-quantized options produce smaller files with reduced color fidelity; the tap-me effect still works.

On the timeline, transparent cover areas trigger Alpha thresholding to fully hide the background. However, solid areas will blend 50/50. Opaque elements in your cover must be backed by opaque pixels in the hidden image, or they will be devoured/turned transparent due to low average alpha.
